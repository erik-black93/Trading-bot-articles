# AI Article Summary Template

## What the article is discussing

- **Brief Overview**: 2-3 sentences describing the main topic and scope
- **Key Focus Areas**: Bullet points of the primary concepts or methodologies covered
- **Scope**: What specific domain (e.g., quantitative research, regime modeling, strategy validation) does this address?

## Key learned material

### 1. [Primary Concept/Theme]
- Specific technical details and best practices
- Actionable insights from the article
- Code patterns or methodologies (if applicable)

### 2. [Secondary Concept/Theme]
- Technical deep-dives
- Common pitfalls or gotchas
- Performance considerations

### 3. [Tertiary Concept/Theme]
- Additional relevant frameworks
- Integration points
- Validation approaches

### 4. [Fourth Concept/Theme]
- Supporting methodologies
- Practical applications
- Industry standards

### 5. [Fifth Concept/Theme]
- Edge cases or limitations
- Advanced variations
- Research frontiers

## How this helps build a profitable trading bot / system

- **Problem Solved**: What challenge does this address in automated trading?
- **Direct Applications**: 1-2 specific ways to apply these concepts
- **Risk Mitigation**: How does this reduce overfitting, curve-fitting, or other common pitfalls?
- **Scalability**: How does this approach scale with market conditions or portfolio size?
- **Operational Benefits**: Monitoring, safety, or production advantages

## Practical takeaways for implementation

- Actionable implementation steps (numbered list)
- Code patterns or pseudocode if relevant
- Configuration recommendations
- Testing and validation approaches

## Notes

- Limitations or caveats of the approach
- When this method might not apply
- Suggested follow-up research or deeper dives
- Connection to other trading concepts

---

## Summary Guidelines for AI Models

**Content to Include:**
- Quantitative metrics and performance indicators
- Statistical methods and validation techniques
- Code-level implementation details
- Risk management frameworks
- Production-grade safeguards

**Content to Exclude:**
- Marketing or promotional language
- Anecdotal trading stories without evidence
- Irrelevant biographical information
- Off-topic commentary or opinions
- Unsubstantiated claims about returns

**Tone & Style:**
- Technical and precise
- Focused on what is actionable and testable
- Evidence-based recommendations
- Links concepts to practical trading bot implementation
